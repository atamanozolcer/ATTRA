We cut, We finish, We deliver. On time, every time.

We produce and supply natural stone — marble, travertine, limestone — for wholesale distributors around the world.

Backed by decades of experience and a modern production setup, we shape stone for reliable, large-scale supply.

Our surfaces are available in over 10 finishings: honed, brushed, bush-hammered, flamed, deep-flamed, sandblasted, tumbled, polished, sea-washed, and custom combinations.

We are able to deliver surfaces with thicknesses as low as 1 cm — a level of precision few suppliers can offer.

We ship worldwide — consistently and fast — powered by our logistics partner MTS. In many cases, delivery to Central Europe takes as little as 2–3 days.
